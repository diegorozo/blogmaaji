How to make a goodbye 2016 ritual…

-Make 2017 a maajical new year, full of love, gratitude & happiness with this easy ritual!

10…9…8… ...3…2….1 ... Happy New Year!!! Ok.. we might not be there yet but the countdown has officially begun to welcome a fabulous new year! And because we want to make sure that we all start 2017 with a good dose of #MAAJINESS we have brainstormed with some of our #RealMermaids across the globe to create a special kit that you can make yourself and share with your friends and loved ones…Be warned that some of the rituals may sound a little crazy if you haven’t tried them before…but like someone said…what does no harm is a charm…so we say give it a try and believe in it and mostly get excited and make the most out of 2017!

Now let´s get started! Grab a little box, get your MAAJI Weekender bag ready (actually empty will do the trick), get a candle…a good scent always adds the extra spark and sets the mood ;), some coins…(we do admit chocolate ones are our favorites!), some matches, paper and something to write with, 2 small bottles of champagne and 2 beautiful bracelets…because sharing the good stuff will not only make Karma be good to you but also have way more fun! And last but not least…(if you don’t already own one) get a yellow MAAJI!

So you are probably wondering why on earth would you need any of that stuff…but hear us out and give it a shot, because we have been doing it for years and we do feel it like it definitely sets a good vibe to start the new year!

On new year’s wear your fabulous yellow MAAJI paired with a sparkling outfit, which that yellow will bring you good luck and prosperity for the new year. Then grab the piece of paper and write your wishes away for 2017; we also like to write on a separate piece of paper what we were grateful for this year, light the matches and burn the old and light the candle to honor your new wishes, some people do like to keep their wishes in a safe place (that’s why we suggest the box) to keep reminding and inspiring yourself throughout the year. Put your coins in your pockets, or bag to attract and have money all year round. Then, when the clock starts ticking and midnight arrives, and the ball drops…(this is when a little bit of multitasking is needed) grab your champagne! And simultaneously kiss, hug, smile, greet eachother…and CHEER…For the New year!! Once you have enjoyed a glass of champagne take your empty weekender bag and wherever you are…go around..take a loop around the house, the hotel, the beach…you name it…just grab that bag and run a little because you want to make sure you will travel to #maajic places next year! Once you are back get your breath back and share your bracelet with that special person…your friend, your sister, your boyfriend, your relative… and show them how much you appreciate them and wish to continue sharing with them a wonderful new year ahead!

To all of you #RealMermaids we wish you a 2017 filled with love, happiness and gratitude…and of course a lot of #MAAJINESS and charming experiences to keep sharing with us!
