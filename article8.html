There´s A Die Dreaming (Morir Soñando) Drink…

INGREDIENTS: -3 CUPS OF MILK
-SUGAR TO TASTE
-2 CUPS OF ICE CUBES OR CRUSHED ICE
-2 CUPS OF ORANGE JUICE
-2 OR 3 CARAMBOLOS

DIRECTIONS
1. Mix the milk and sugar to taste. Stir until all the sugar has dissolved.
2. Put the milk in the freezer for some minutes until it is cold.
3. Add the ice cubes and the orange juice and stir.
4. Serve and enjoy this refreshing unexpected combination! It tastes delicious!

Maajical chocolate rice krispies bars!

YOU´LL NEED:
-Your own #RealMermaid DUST to add the touch of MAAJIC
- 1⁄4 cup butter
- 10 ounces marshmallows
-6cups Cocoa Krispies

DIRECTIONS
Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium low heat. Add the cocoa rice krispies and marshmallows and stir until the marshmallow have completely melted and the mixture is smooth. Remove from the heat and pour the mix over a buttered bowl. Let sit at room temperature until set. Sprinkle your MAAJICAL dust and cut into squares! Makes about 16 MAAJICAL SQUARES!
