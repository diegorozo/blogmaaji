Meet our #realmermaid of the month:
Sofia Gomez Uribe!


We are sure you are going to love (like we already do!) getting to know Sofi, our Colombian freedive and South American record holder! Yes, she trains hard, but she also loves desserts….

What is your perfect VACATION spot?

It has to be somewhere I can combine my PASSION for the OCEAN and my love for the MOUNTAINS, being away from the sea is not easy.

Favorite underwater SPECIES?

SHARKS! I love swimming with them because there is where I can see that they are not crazy human-eating animals, but these amazing creatures that won’t bother you if you don’t mess with them.

Pancakes or waffles?

PANCAKES! With lots of maple syrup!

ICE-CREAM flavors?

Cookie Dough and French Vanilla! Can I have one of each please?

Favorite DESSERT?

Chocolate Lava cake with Vanilla ice cream

Give us an #EARTHWARRIOR advise….

Whenever you can do something to REDUCE your CONSUMPTION of non-recyclable things, do it. Whenever you can stop using plastic, do it. Whatever you do, think about the impact that it has on the planet. There are thing you can’t change, but there are thing you can do to help avoid destroying the environment!

Top 5 most beautiful BEACHES you have been to?

The Bahamas, Los Roques (Venezuela), Choroní (Venezuela), Wavine Cyrique (Dominica), Roatán (Honduras)

What FEELINGS and THOUGHTS come to mind when you are deep in the water?

I feel PEACE, and I also feel we are very VULNERABLE and small, and we have to be GRATEFUL for what we have and live our life to the fullest.

Do you have any RITUALS before competitions?

I like listening to MUSIC.

When you are not in the water, what’s your FAVORITE thing to do?

I love riding my BIKE everywhere.

Most #MAAJICAL moment in your life?

The first time I swam with SHARKS

What is the CRAZIEST thing you have seen underwater?

Once we were swimming in the reef in Roatán and all of the sudden, a big group of DOLPHINS came swimming super close to us. It was magical to see 30 or 40 free dolphins swimming and playing with us

What is your favorite CHRISTMAS tradition?

Once we were swimming in the reef in Roatán and all of the sudden, a big group of DOLPHINS came swimming super close to us. It was magical to see 30 or 40 free dolphins swimming and playing with us

What are you most GRATEFUL for?

My FAMILY and the fact I can live DOING what I LOVE

Your favorite MAAJI ever?

My beautiful RASHGUARDS!

Triangle or bustier?

TRIANGLE

Short dress or long dress?

SHORT, i’m too short to wear long dresses :P

Thank you Sofi for sharing with all of us your #MAAJIC and your sparkling personality!
