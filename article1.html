MAAJIC BEHIND MAAJI

Meet Amali & Manu, two sisters that dreamed of creating MAGIC…and MAAJI was born!

¨Watch the video to meet Amali and Manu, the charming sisters who are behind our unique pieces and what we stand for!¨

Hi there #RealMermaids! We are beyond excited to continue spreading our #MAAJIC and love through our new blog! Here, we´ll be sharing exciting news and adventures, not only about our little art pieces but also we want you to help us continue building our troop of #EARTHWARRIORS and strengthen our commitment with our society and with our beloved planet EARTH! We thought a good way to inaugurate our blog was by sharing the #MAAJIC that takes place behind MAAJI.

“Our art pieces are always a canvas for innovation and whimsicality; and so they become a world of eclecticism”

As many as you know our inspiration comes primarily from our beautiful country: COLOMBIA. This is where our home offices are located. A country we invite you to explore and see through our eyes; filled with beauty, charm, with a wide range of colors and rainbows, big hearted magicians and artisans who with their creativity and magical energy have given new ways to weave our dreams through diversity and passion. All of which continuously is feeding our team with ideas to create newness, but also on how to stimulate and support our commitment to contribute to the social development of our country and our natural environment as well.

Our designs are always a canvas for innovation and whimsicality; and so they become a world of eclecticism: mixing prints, colors, and textures. Resulting into the creation of a real expression of art and enchanted pieces to be worn and shared across the world! And we thank all of you for sharing your #RealMermaid moments with us, because those as well, inspire us to keep crafting our #MAAJIC!

“We definitely owe our MAAJIC to our wonderful and exemplary #RealMermaids”

Also, we definitely owe our #EartWarriors, who have really strengthen and embody our commitment to preserve our Oceans and Seas as well as our society. We are all continuously seeking ways to give back to our giving planet, and surely we invite all of you to join our forces wherever you are in the world to make a positive impact on the environment as well as the community. Our platform of "Planting an Ocean of Trees", has given us the opportunity to build a Maaji’s forest of 90.000 trees, and we aim to keep on growing.
