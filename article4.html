Yes! Making the wolrd a better place is possible!

Free Diving: their passion, The Ocean: their commitment!

How remarkable and inspiring it is to see someone not only do what they love, but also be committed with the ENVIRONMENT and invite others to join them? This is why we joined forces with our #RealMermaid and an exemplary #EarthWarrior Sofi Gomez and the amazing Blue Element competition team!

The competition and beach cleanup took place at Dominica Island at the beginning of November. There, Sofi not only set a new Continental and Pan-American record but also went the deepest any woman has been in the history of free-diving! That is definitely #Maajical and we celebrate her new achievements!

Besides setting new records she also promoted our #EarthWarriors program: cleaning beaches and giving back to our planet while connecting with the community, all which could happen thanks to the amazing and remarkable support of the Blue Element team. Big thanks to all of them for making it all happen!

“she did not only set a new Continental and Pan-American record but also went the deepest any woman has been in the history of free-diving!”

Like they said the oceans are part of our lives! And we all need to promote healthy eco-systems and healthy living. That involves creating good relationships with the community we live in so we can give back to them and therefore to our planet! Even small actions show we care and give example and inspiration to others! So let’s unite forces and find ways to be extraordinary #EarthWarriors!

And to learn more about our #RealMermaid Sofia read our conversation with her in our Real Mermaid section! Also, If you are into diving make sure to visit the Blue Element page at https://www.facebook.com/blueelementfreediving/
